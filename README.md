ApplyFlow AI – AI-Powered Job Application Platform
Overview

ApplyFlow AI is a modern full-stack job application management platform that helps users organize, track, and optimize their job search process.

The platform enables users to manage job applications, visualize progress through a Kanban workflow, upload and organize resumes, and monitor application analytics through an interactive dashboard. It has been engineered with a scalable architecture to support future AI-powered capabilities such as Resume-to-JD Matching, ATS optimization, Cover Letter Generation, and Interview Preparation.
